A versatile professional with diverse experience in manufacturing and engineering industries, coupled with a strong educational background in accounting. Participation in the Full-Stack Web Development bootcamp at Dicoding Academy expanded my insights into the world of software development, challenging me to explore further in this field.

I have a strong desire to develop skills in software engineering and contribute to the development of innovative products and services. With dedication to learning and growth, I aim to leverage my previous experience and newfound education to achieve a sustainable career as a software engineer.
